Champions Heritage Children Foundation promotes Nigerian cultural heritage through arts, crafts, and creative workshops for children aged 5 - 16years.

Explore Our Programs

Welcome to Champions Heritage

At Champions Heritage Children Foundation, we believe that culture is the foundation of identity and creativity is the pathway to the future. Our mission is to preserve and promote Nigerian cultural heritage while empowering children through arts and crafts education.

Through our comprehensive workshops and exhibitions, we provide children aged 5 - 16years with hands-on experience in traditional and contemporary arts, helping them connect with their roots while developing essential life skills.

We work with agencies, corporate organizations, and communities across Lagos State to create meaningful cultural experiences that inspire the next generation of creative leaders.
